Our mission

Open Mind is a community-based space to find and contribute answers to questions in the Psychology, Social work and Speech therapy fields challenges. We know the struggle of finding support in field where all information is privet, and we are morally obligated to this privacy. We know how hard it is to find advice, understanding or just someone to laugh to field base jokes. So we are committed to bring together specialists from all around the world and create the community that we deserve. Find support, understanding, friendship. Find your village. The core focus of Open Mind is to serve its members through educational events, networking opportunities, resources, publication and promotion of research, and mentoring the next generation of social and personality psychologists.
